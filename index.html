About Me

Hi Everyone!

I studied Mathematics and Computer Science at Loyola University Chicago. As an undergraduate, I did a research project that combined both my interests. In May 2017, I graduated Loyola with a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics with a minor in Computer Science.

Following Loyola, I pursued a Master of Science in Mathematics, graduating from DePaul University Chicago in June 2019. During my time at DePaul, I worked as a teaching assistant for Intermediate Algebra, Calculus I-III, Linear Algebra, and two graduate courses, namely Fields and Galois Theory and Partial Differential Equations. Now, I work as a Professional Tutor at Oakton Community College, tutoring mathematics.

Since graduating DePaul, I have reconnected with my computer science interests. I have self-taught myself JavaScript by reading and doing exercises on freeCodeCamp, HackerRank, LeetCode, and AlgoExpert.

This past Summer, June 2020 - August 2020, I did two full-time trainings, hosted by Revature, that covered JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Java, Angular, SQL, and AWS concepts. I recently passed a JavaScript certification from W3Schools.

I built this site to practice showcase some of my work as well as discuss my journey as a Software Engineer.
